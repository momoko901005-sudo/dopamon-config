DopaMon Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DopaMon! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Developer: DopaMon
App Name: DopaMon - Task Pet
Platform: iOS

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • User Profile: Trainer name, avatar selection
  • Tasks: Task titles and completion data you create
  • Pet Names: Custom names you give to your DopaMon creatures
  • App Preferences: Language settings, sound preferences

2.2 Health Data (Optional)

If you grant permission, we access your health data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Step Count: To automatically complete step-based tasks
  • Sleep Data: To track sleep-related goals

Important:

  • Health data is never sent to our servers
  • All health data stays on your device and in your iCloud (if enabled)
  • We do not sell or share health data with any third parties
  • You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version (for compatibility)
  • App Usage Analytics: Features used, session duration, app crashes (via Firebase Analytics)
  • In-App Purchase Data: Purchase verification (handled by Apple, not stored by us)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide and improve the App experience:

  • Core Functionality: Track your tasks, hatch eggs, manage your DopaMon collection
  • Personalization: Display your creatures, show personalized dialogues based on pet personality
  • Cloud Sync: Sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add requested features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All your app data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage (UserDefaults)
  • Data includes: user profile, tasks, pets, eggs, inventory, and game progress

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud, your data is encrypted and synced via Apple iCloud
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data - it's protected by your Apple ID
  • You can disable iCloud sync anytime in iOS Settings

4.3 Analytics

  • We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand app usage
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to Firebase

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5.1 Apple Services

  • HealthKit: Access health data (with your permission)
  • iCloud: Sync data across devices (optional)
  • StoreKit: Process in-app purchases

Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5.2 Google Firebase

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics
  • Firebase Remote Config: Dynamic app configuration

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your data.

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly choose to share (e.g., sharing a pet card on social media)
  • Service Providers: Apple and Google (as described above) to operate the App
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App
  • Export: Use the Transfer Code feature to export your data
  • Delete: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to permanently delete all app data
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Contact us to disable Firebase Analytics for your account
  • Revoke HealthKit Access: iOS Settings → Privacy → Health → DopaMon → Turn Off

8. Children's Privacy

DopaMon is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

DopaMon is available worldwide. Your data may be processed in:

  • Your Country: Data stored locally on your device
  • United States: Firebase servers (Google) may process analytics data
  • Your iCloud Region: If you enable iCloud sync

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Showing an in-app notification
  • Posting the updated policy on this page

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: momoko901005@gmail.com
